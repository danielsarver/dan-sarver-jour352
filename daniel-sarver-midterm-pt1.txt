2. Describe the purpose of a DIV tag.

A DIV tag is an html element that works as a container to group together different HTML elements so they can be formatted by CSS.

10. Why do we use media queries?

Media queries are used to implement a responsive design and change the style of an element at different screen sizes. 

11. What is the main difference between a serif and a sans-serif font?

Serif font has little decorative lines called serifs on the letters that give more of an elequent feel. Sans-serif doesn't have the serifs on the letters, giving them a more modern and readable feel.
